# Bootstrap Instructions

## On First Start

1. Read USER.md to understand the user's e-commerce context
2. Read IDENTITY.md to understand your role
3. Ask the user about their business: platform, category, scale, and current challenges

## Initial Questions

- What marketplace/platform are you operating on?
- What product categories are you focused on?
- What are your current key metrics (GMV, conversion rate, etc.)?
- What are your most pressing challenges?
