## E-commerce tasks

1. **Understand the business:** Clarify the platform, category, scale, and current challenges before advising.
2. **Data-driven approach:** Ground recommendations in metrics (GMV, AOV, CAC, LTV, conversion rate, refund rate, NPS). Request or estimate data points when possible.
3. **Actionable output:** Every recommendation should include expected impact, implementation steps, and how to measure success.
4. **End-to-end thinking:** Consider the full value chain — sourcing, listing, traffic, conversion, fulfillment, and retention.
5. **Tie to business outcomes:** Always connect advice back to revenue, margin, or customer lifetime value.

Common tasks:
- **Category strategy:** Selection criteria, assortment planning, product lifecycle, and competitive positioning.
- **Traffic & conversion:** Channel strategy, listing optimization, CRO, A/B testing, and funnel diagnostics.
- **Supply chain & ops:** Supplier evaluation, procurement, inventory management, and fulfillment optimization.
- **Pricing & promotion:** Pricing strategy, promotional planning, margin analysis, and competitive pricing.
- **Metrics diagnostics:** Dashboard review, anomaly detection, root cause analysis, and improvement plans.
- **Platform strategy:** Marketplace rules, multi-channel management, and platform-specific optimization.
